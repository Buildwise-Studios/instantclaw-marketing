# InstantClaw Design System

## Carousel Design Rules

### 1. Format: Aggressively Fill 4:5 Space
**CRITICAL:** All Instagram carousel slides MUST aggressively use the 4:5 format (1080×1350px). Never leave excessive empty space.

- **Typography:** Go BIG. Headlines should be massive (text-7xl minimum, often text-8xl+).
- **Logos:** Size up significantly. No tiny icons.
- **Elements:** Fill vertical and horizontal space. Elements should nearly touch edges.
- **Padding:** Minimal. Use p-6 or p-8 maximum, never excessive margins.
- **Density:** Prefer denser layouts over sparse/minimal.

### 2. Typography
- **Font Family:** Space Grotesk (NOT Inter, Roboto, or Arial)
- **Headlines:** Bold, gradient text using brand colors
- **Hierarchy:** Clear size contrast between headline, subhead, and body

### 3. Brand Colors
- **Primary Gold:** #D4A574 (warm gold)
- **Accent Red:** #c53131 (brand red)
- **Dark Text:** #1D1C1D
- **Background:** #F8F7F5 (warm off-white)
- **Gradient:** `from-[#1D1C1D] via-[#c53131] via-[#D4A574] to-[#1D1C1D]`

### 4. Buttons (CTA Style)
Use the ShimmerButton style from the app:
- **Background:** #D4A574 (warm gold)
- **Shape:** Pill/rounded-full (not boxy)
- **Border:** border-white/20
- **Effect:** Shimmer animation
- **Shadow:** shadow-lg with subtle white inset
- **Text:** Dark text (#1D1C1D), bold

### 5. Cards & Containers
- **Border Radius:** rounded-2xl (not rounded-xl)
- **Shadow:** Subtle reddish tint: `rgba(197, 49, 49, 0.15)`
- **Background:** White or warm off-white

### 6. Icons
- **Background:** Solid warm gold (#D4A574) — NOT gradient
- **Size:** Large (w-14 h-14 minimum for feature icons)
- **Style:** Rounded containers with subtle shadow

### 7. Layout Principles
- **Asymmetry:** Prefer asymmetrical layouts over centered
- **Tension:** Create visual interest with offset elements
- **Flow:** Guide the eye with positioning, not just center alignment

### 8. OpenClaw Branding
- **Mention OpenClaw:** Include "OpenClaw" or "Powered by OpenClaw" on cover slides
- **Consistency:** Reference the app design for buttons, shadows, and styling

### 9. Platform Logos (Telegram/Slack)
- **Size:** Large avatars (w-16 h-16 or w-20 h-20)
- **Style:** Circular with white background, subtle border
- **Label:** Include "Works with" or "Available on" text

---

## Before/After Checklist

Before finalizing any carousel slide, verify:

- [ ] Is the 4:5 space aggressively filled? (No excessive empty areas)
- [ ] Is the headline BIG enough?
- [ ] Are logos/icons sized appropriately (not tiny)?
- [ ] Is Space Grotesk being used?
- [ ] Are brand colors applied correctly?
- [ ] Do buttons match the ShimmerButton style?
- [ ] Is there visual tension/asymmetry (not just centered)?
- [ ] Is OpenClaw mentioned where appropriate?

---

## Example: Good vs Bad

**BAD:** Centered, small text, lots of whitespace, Inter font, tiny icons
**GOOD:** Asymmetrical, massive text, filled space, Space Grotesk, large icons, minimal padding
